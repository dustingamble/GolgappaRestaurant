Indian Street Food

Our Story

Golgappa is a modern Indian kitchen created to bring the smells and tastes from the streets of India to your plate. We believe in fast and flavorful meals using only the freshest ingredients, while our food pays homage to all the hard working street merchants across the whole of India.

Enjoy

Whether you're in a hurry or want to take your time, Golgappa invites you to enjoy some fresh pakoras, a large helping of vegan masala dosa, and wash it down with one of our refreshing Aam Lassis.
